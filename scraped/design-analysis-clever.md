## Key Design Elements

### 1. Hero Section
**Headline:** "Compare top local real estate agents. Save thousands on commission."
**CTA:** "Get Started" button (prominent)
**Subtext:** "Sell with a top agent for just a 1.5% listing fee. Clever's service is free with no obligation. Get matched in minutes."

**Key Takeaway:** Clear value proposition with specific number (1.5%) and benefit (save thousands)

### 2. Social Proof (Trust Bar)
- **"23,000 people"** have bought or sold with Clever
- **"$150M saved"** in real estate fees
- **Press logos:** Yahoo Finance, Realtor.com, Forbes, NBC, USA Today, NY Post, Fox Business
- **Trustpilot:** "Excellent" rating with 3,500+ reviews

**Key Takeaway:** Heavy emphasis on credibility through numbers, press, and reviews

### 3. Three-Column Value Props

#### Column 1: Network
- "A nationwide network of 14,000 top agents"
- Partners with RE/MAX, eXp, and major brands
- Agents maintain exceptional reviews

#### Column 2: Rates
- "The best rates in real estate"
- "1.5% listing fee ($3,000 minimum)"
- "Half their typical fee"
- "Clever sellers save $7,000 on average!"

#### Column 3: Free Service
- "Free with no obligation"
- "Sign up and compare agent matches in minutes"
- "Choose best fit, request more matches, or walk away"
- Clear explanation of how they make money

**Key Takeaway:** Transparency, specific numbers, risk-free messaging

## Design Patterns to Implement

### 1. **Specific Numbers Everywhere**
- 1.5% fee (vs generic "low commission")
- $7,000 average savings
- 23,000 customers served
- $150M saved
- 14,000 agents
- 3,500+ reviews

**Implementation:** Add specific numbers to Alchemy site:
- "Save $7,000+ on average"
- "500+ homes purchased"
- "15+ years in business"
- "$50M+ paid to homeowners"

### 2. **Trust Badge Bar**
- Press logo bar immediately after hero
- Trustpilot/review widget prominently displayed

**Implementation:** Add trust elements:
- Better Business Bureau rating
- Years in business
- License number prominently
- Customer testimonial count

### 3. **"How It Works" with Numbers**
Instead of generic steps, show:
- "Get matched in minutes" (time)
- "Compare 2-3 agents" (quantity)
- "Close in 7 days" (specific timeline)

### 4. **Free/No Obligation Messaging**
- "Free with no obligation" repeated multiple times
- Clear explanation of how they make money
- "Walk away at any time"

**Implementation:** Add to Alchemy:
- "No obligation cash offer"
- "Free property evaluation"
- "No fees or commissions - we explain how we make offers"

### 5. **Average Savings vs. Comparison**
Instead of just "fast" or "convenient", show:
- "Save $7,000 on average"
- Comparison table showing traditional vs. their service

**Implementation:** Already have comparison table - enhance with dollar amounts

## Content Structure

1. **Hero** - Big promise + CTA
2. **Trust Bar** - Numbers + Press logos
3. **Three Benefits** - Network, Rates, Free
4. **How It Works** - 3-4 numbered steps
5. **Agent Matching Process** - Detailed explanation
6. **Testimonials** - Real stories with photos
7. **FAQ** - Common objections handled
8. **Final CTA** - Repeat the offer

---

## Color & Visual Style

- **Clean, professional** - Lots of white space
- **Blue as primary** - Trust, professionalism
- **Green for CTAs** - "Go" action color
- **Press logos in grayscale** - Professional look
- **Large, readable fonts** - Easy to scan

---

## Copy Style

- **Direct and benefit-focused**
- **Specific numbers over vague claims**
- **Risk-reversal language** ("free", "no obligation", "walk away")
- **Social proof woven throughout**
- **Conversational tone** but professional
