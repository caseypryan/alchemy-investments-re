## Competitive Gaps & Opportunities

### 1. Schema Markup (CRITICAL OPPORTUNITY)
**Finding**: None of the analyzed competitors use proper structured data
**Opportunity**: Implement comprehensive schema for:
- LocalBusiness with NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
- RealEstateAgent
- FAQPage for FAQ content
- Review/AggregateRating
- Organization
- BreadcrumbList

**Expected Impact**: Rich snippets in SERPs, higher CTR, better local pack rankings

### 2. Content Depth
**Finding**: Competitors focus on sales pages, limited educational content
**Opportunity**: Create comprehensive blog covering:
- Process guides ("How to Sell Your House Fast")
- Market insights ("Las Vegas Real Estate Market 2026")
- Problem-solving content ("Stopping Foreclosure," "Selling Inherited Property")
- Neighborhood guides (Summerlin, Henderson, etc.)
- Comparison content ("Cash Offer vs Traditional Sale")

**Expected Impact**: Long-tail keyword rankings, authority building, more organic entry points

### 3. Trust & Social Proof
**Finding**: Limited integration of reviews/testimonials on homepages
**Opportunity**:
- Embed Google reviews directly on homepage
- Add video testimonials
- Display credentials prominently (license, BBB, associations)
- Show transaction volume/years in business
- Add live chat for instant trust

**Expected Impact**: Higher conversion rates, reduced bounce rate

### 4. Local SEO Optimization
**Finding**: Generic Las Vegas targeting, no neighborhood specificity
**Opportunity**:
- Create individual landing pages for:
  - Summerlin home buyers
  - Henderson cash offers
  - North Las Vegas property sales
  - Boulder City real estate
  - Pahrump house buyers
- Add local landmarks/references in content
- Target "near me" searches
- Build local citations

**Expected Impact**: Capture neighborhood-specific searches, dominate local pack

### 5. Technical SEO
**Finding**: Mixed performance - some use optimization, others don't
**Opportunity**:
- Next.js for optimal performance (SSG/ISR)
- <3s page load time
- Core Web Vitals optimization
- Image optimization (WebP)
- Mobile-first indexing ready

**Expected Impact**: Better rankings (page speed is ranking factor), higher conversions
